# Sticky footers

Creating a footer which sits at the bottom of the container or viewport when the content is shorter.

## Grid

<div className={styles.wrapperWithGrid}>
  <header className={styles.pageHeader}>This is the header</header>
  <main className={styles.pageBody}>
    <p>The footer sticks to the bottom even though this paragraph is short. Add
      content to this editable area to see the footer push down when needed to
      fit the content.</p>
  </main>
  <footer className={styles.pageFooter}>Sticky footer</footer>
</div>
